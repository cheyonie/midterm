About Me

My name is Chey and I will be your braider! I am originally from California and that is where my journey of braiding began. I was a 6th grader at Washington Elementary School creating designs in my friend's hair for fun! I am located on the southwest side in a luxury suite. Punctuality is extremely important to me, so I have a starting time guarantee. If any changes need to occur, I will provide you with more than a 24 hour notice. With my promise, I hope that you will be able to provide me the same! Please do not schedule anything important the day of your important. Quality means the world to me, so I would like to make sure I am able to focus on giving you a beautiful style without feeling rushed!
